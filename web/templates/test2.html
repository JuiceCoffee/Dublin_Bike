BICYCLE GO!!!

Cycling is a very common form of transportation in Dublin, which not only contributes to lessening traffic congestion but also lowers carbon emissions and enhances the quality of life for city dwellers. The Dublin City Council is dedicated to creating a bicycle-friendly infrastructure, including more bike lanes and services for renting and sharing bicycles, including Dublin Bikes.


The purpose of the service offered by our website is to enhance the user experience of renting a public bicycle, contribute significantly to the ecological health and integrity of Berlin's public bicycle fleet, and offer technical assistance for Dublin's green mobility initiative.

Predict Bikes

We have the best industry-leading accuracy record of over 99.3% when estimating the number of bicycles at a certain bicycle station over the next five days! In order to link real-time meteorological data (humidity, temperature, light sensitivity, and other influencing factors even consider three decimal places) and create a comprehensive closure prediction chain, we analyze massive multi-dimensional data, use deep learning to create a unique calculation model, and finally perform the analysis.
